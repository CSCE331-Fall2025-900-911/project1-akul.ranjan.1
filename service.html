Peer Mentoring

I believe growth accelerates when learning is shared. I mentor peers by walking through debugging workflows, trading interview strategies, and demystifying the everyday tools of software work. Teaching is a forcing function for clarity; when I explain a topic, I notice my own gaps and go fix them.

STEM Outreach

I also support beginner-friendly outreach activities. That has included setting up development environments, writing concise guides to remove early friction, and pairing with new programmers to build confidence through small wins. The goal is to make the first steps into programming welcoming and safe to explore.

Open Resources

Finally, I curate open resources—short notes and mini-tutorials that emphasize accessibility. That means plain language, keyboard-friendly navigation, and examples that are small but complete. I’ve seen how a well-timed snippet or checklist can unblock someone; sharing that kind of material is a simple way to multiply impact.
